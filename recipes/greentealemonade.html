Green Tea Lemonade

Description

A light and refreshing drink that will quench your thirst.

Enjoy a green tea lemonade on a hot day. This drink can help keep you cool and give you some energy with the caffeine from the tea.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Green tea bag or matcha powder
  • Lemonade
  • Ice
  • Mint Leaves *optional

Steps

  1. Prepare your green tea according to direction
  2. In a jug or pitcher, combine one part lemonade and one part green tea
  3. Taste your mixture and adjust to your liking
  4. Pour over ice in a glass and enjoy!!
